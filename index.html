cApps is a framework for businesses, developers, end users and the community in general to create and use a variety of different apps that are hosted in a Software as a Service environment. By making use of the Microsoft Azure platform it is simple and quick to not only create new apps that take advantage of the robustness, security, and scalibility that Azure provides, but for businesses and end users to sign up and begin using productivity enhancing apps in just minutes. In addition, you can leverage the Office 365 and/or SharePoint Online platforms to drive increases in communication, searchability, and teamwork throughout the entire company.

cApps combines a number of exciting and powerful technologies to provide a solution to that elusive goal of monetizing your companies IT and business knowledge. How many apps has your company developed in-house that everyone knows would be quite useful for a variety of other businesses but it just doesn't seem worth the efforts to actually branch off into selling these solutions? With the cApps framework you can package these apps into a deployable solution that lives in the cloud, can be supported by experienced developers outside of your organization so your team is not distracted by support issues, and delivers a monthly revenue stream directly to your bottom line.

FAQ

What technologies does cApp use?
The cApp framework leverages the Windows Azure cloud from Microsoft as well as the Microsoft Office 365 and SharePoint Online.
How long does it take to develop a cApp?
Because the cApp framework uses the Azure cloud, much of the development plumbing has been done for you by the engineers at Microsoft. So creating a new cApp can easily be done with just 1-2 hours worth of development.
How can I monetize a cApp I develop?
After a cApp has been developed and published to the staging environment, a member of our team will review it based on a list of criteria that we have developed to ensure that all cApps are of high quality and implement robust security among other considertaions. Once a cApp has been approved it goes into the store and is available for customers to install in their portal. Pricing is set by the developer of the cApp with a 40-70% fee being assesed depending upon a number of factors.
